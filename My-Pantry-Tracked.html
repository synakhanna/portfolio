My Pantry Tracked

About

Whether it is brand-new items or leftovers, sometimes people have too many groceries and ingredients to keep track of. As a result, several people end up with spoiled foods which they had forgotten about. As a solution to handle many pantry items, I created a web application called My Pantry Tracked, which can be used to track inventory items in your pantry. You can add items, remove items, or search for items within the tracking app.
