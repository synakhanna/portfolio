CollegeGenie

About

CollegeGenie is an AI-powered platform which helps students find the right colleges, streamline applications, and achieve success in their future college journey. CollegeGenie makes the college application process easier and more efficient by helping you to:

  • Find the perfect college based on your interests, location, and academic goals using our AI-powered search.
  • Receive personalized guidance and tips for every part of your application, from essays to recommendation letters.
  • Get feedback on your college essays from our AI tool that provides suggestions for improving clarity, tone, and impact.
  • Discover scholarships that you qualify for based on your background and academic achievements.
  • Prepare for college interviews with AI-generated practice questions and tips to help you feel confident.
  • Build your network by connecting with other fellow students.
