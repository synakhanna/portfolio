CodeFlash

About

There are several challenges when it comes to tech prep. The lack of interactive study tools, difficulty in memorizing critical programming elements, and time-consuming traditional study methods all contribute to failures of exams and technical interviews. The solution is... CodeFlash! CodeFlash is an organized, efficient, and interactive tool which utilizes the power of Artificial Intelligence to generate flashcards with questions and answers for various coding and programming languages. It covers several topics such as HTML, CSS, Python, JavaScript, Java, C, C++, and SQL. CodeFlash allows for quick and repetitive learning, which saves time when studying as it is faster than other studying methods. This product is also convenient for users as it is available to use at anytime, anywhere, on any device.
