My Pantry Tracked

About

5 boroughs. 700+ hotels. 4,000+ attractions. And over 27,000 restaurants. New York City is the most populous city in the Unites States, and therefore has several places to visit. When I moved to New York, I realized there are endless options for places to go to—whether it is places to eat or sites to visit. This made decision-making very difficult and time-consuming for me. I thought about how others may struggle to make decisions too, so I compiled data on some of the top restaurants, attractions, and hotels from each borough and created a New York City guide which tourists and residents of NYC could use.
