About

As I have studied the principles of UX design, I applied my design and coding skills to create an informational website based on my knowledge of UX design. I decided to give this website a modern twist in the design aspect, so that it could be different than the traditional informational site. Moreover, I added some playful elements such as changing the font of the title and changing images. On the very top of the site (next to the title), you can see the day of the week, which changes everyday.
