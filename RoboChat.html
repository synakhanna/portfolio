About

RobotChat is an Artificial Intelligence (AI) powered program designed to simulate conversations with humans as a human-like bot. It can serve various purposes, including being a chat buddy, being helpful for information retrieval, and giving personal assistance, etc. Made with OpenAI’s API, RoboChat is very knowledgable. The AI is capable of detecting over 100+ languages, and is very helpful to users due to its extensive database of information. In order to ensure a personalized user experience, several elements are customizable. You can switch between dark/light mode, you can customize the name, and you change the color theme of the chat. Lastly, RoboChat is a secure app. There are two ways to use it: You can create an account with an email and password, or you could sign in with Google.
