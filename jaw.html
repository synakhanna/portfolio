About

I started a nonprofit organization called Journey Around the World (JAW) to help underprivileged communities around the world. We have hosted several fundraisers and donation drives in order to support those in need. After a few years of working hard, volunteers from the nonprofit (including myself) flew to Cambodia to teach students in a rural village near Kep, where the kids did not have access to education. As we started planning more events and volunteer trips, our nonprofit began to recieve several collaboration requests. To help increase the impact and inform more people of our work, I created a website showcasing our projects and explaining how people can help.
